*** PLAYER UP ***

Have you got what it takes to help rid cycle space of the evil invaders?

In order to join, all you need is a camera and a Twitter account. Simply capture an invader in a cycle space and post it with the hashtag #FreeTheCycleLanes or #KissTheGatesGoodbye.

The most difficult part of the game is finding a cycle path due to Ireland's poor record of developing quality cycle infrastructure. Once you have located a cycle path you can be almost guaranteed to find an invader in it!

Points are awarded for each capture, as well as extra points if you GeoTag or hashtag your location.

Have what it takes to make the Leader Board?

* SCORE ADVANCE TABLE *

Capture an photo or video of an invader in a cyclelane for 10 points.

Geotag your tweet for an additional 5 points.
This will help to map and identify hotspots.

Add your town, city or county as a hashtag for an additional 5 points.
This will help to group issues for a locality.
